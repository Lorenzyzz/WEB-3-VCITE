Virtual CITE


VISSION

We envision CITE Technical Institute, Inc. to be an educational institution that provides technical training to the less
privileged youth in a manner that is always deeply rooted in moral and work values, strongly linked with
families and industry, and extensively imbued with the entrepreneurial spirit.

MISSION

CITE Technical Institute, Inc. is an institution committed to provide training in technical skills and
entrepreneurship, values formation, health and social services to the less privileged youth, their families, the local
community and the industrial sector of Visayas and Mindanao. We aim to develop in them a high sense of
professionalism and a deep Christian and social oriented spirit.
